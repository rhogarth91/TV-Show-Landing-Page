Which side of the force will you choose?

It is a dark time in the galaxy as the Galactic Empire continues to tighten its grip on the people through oppression and fear, compelling a few brave individuals to band together in resistance. The crew of the starship , The Ghost stands up for those who cannot fight for themselves, providing the spark to ignite a rebellion.
